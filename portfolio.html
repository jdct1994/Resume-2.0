Responsive Web Design (RWD)

All of my projects are designed to deliver an optimal viewing experience on most mobile, tablet and desktop devices. Desktop screen sizes optimized for include: 1920px by 1080px, 1366px by 768px, 1280px by 1024px., 1280px by 800px, and 1024px by 768px. Mobile and tablet screens accounted for include: All iPhones and iPads, Microsoft Surface, Galaxy Tab, Kindle Fire, Microsoft Lumia, Samsung Galaxy, LG, Sony Experia, HTC Phones and much, much more. If you would like your project designed for a specific device or according to specific screen size dimensions, please feel free to let me know, and I will be sure to craft the best experience possible.
